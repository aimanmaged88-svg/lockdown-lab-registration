FAQ

Straight answers

Q01What sports does it cover?＋
Basketball, soccer, MMA, oztag, netball, rugby, AFL, tennis, swimming, running, volleyball — plus an Other / Multi mode for everything else. The engine is sport-agnostic; content packs swap in.
Q02Is my data really private?＋
Athletes can never see each other. There is no feed and no shared leaderboard. Only you and the coach can see your file, and your mental-file entries stay private unless you explicitly share them.
Q03What does the AI coach actually do?＋
It reads your daily check-ins — energy, sleep, soreness, minutes, focus — and turns them into trends, flags and next moves in your sport's language. It educates and motivates; it never diagnoses injuries or replaces a doctor, physio or your real coach.
Q04Does it replace my coach?＋
No — it arms them. Your coach gets a live dashboard of every athlete with search, filters and private notes, so the humans coach and the OS handles the accountability.
Q05What does it cost?＋
Free while we build. Pro features — video analysis, wearables, online programs — will come later. Early athletes lock in founding treatment.
