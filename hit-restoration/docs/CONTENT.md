## The five restoration services (client-approved copy — use verbatim)
1. **Cleaning** — Careful surface cleaning that lifts dirt, fingerprints, and handling residue
   without touching the card's original print or finish.
2. **Dent Removal** — Dents and indentations worked out using controlled, card-safe techniques
   that restore a flat surface without damaging the stock underneath.
3. **Crease Repair** — Creases and folds are carefully reduced, softening the visible line and
   restoring a cleaner overall look.
4. **Corner Repair** — Worn, soft, or rounded corners are rebuilt and sharpened for crisp edges.
5. **Surface Treatment** — A finishing pass addressing scratches, print lines, and general
   surface wear.

## Pricing (confirmed structure)
- **Inspection fee**: $10 AUD flat per card, charged at physical intake. Non-refundable;
  credited toward the final invoice if the customer proceeds; kept if they decline.
- **Restoration fee**: tiered flat base by the card's confirmed intake value, PLUS a tiered percentage of confirmed intake value
  (UPDATED per client feedback July 2026 — % scales with tier):
  - Under $100 → $20 base + 7.5%
  - $100 – $2,000 → $50 base + 10%
  - $2,000 – $10,000 → $120 base + 12.5%
  - $10,000+ → custom quote
- **PSA grading** (UPDATED — confirmed final; PSA-only at launch, CGC/BGS/SGC "coming soon"):
  flat all-in fee per card by speed — Regular (40–50 business days) $225 · Express (20–30) $375 ·
  Super Express (10) $850. Charged before the card ships out.
- **Tier jump** (REPLACES the old 4% upcharge): only if PSA reassesses a card into a higher
  tier after grading — the tier-fee difference + $20 admin. Card ships back once paid.
- **Additional-cost approval (T&C clause 4.6, new)**: extra work found mid-job requires customer
  approval + payment before proceeding — a second checkpoint while In Restoration.
- **Payment flow (staged — nothing charged against a guessed value):**
  1. Submit online — no charge, estimate only.
  2. Card arrives — condition + value confirmed; $10 inspection fee charged.
  3. Firm restoration quote issued on confirmed value — approve & pay before any work begins.
  4. Restoration carried out.
  5. If grading: the flat all-in PSA fee (by chosen speed) charged before the card ships out.
  6. Card returns graded: any tier-jump difference + $20 admin settled; card ships back once paid.
- Store shipping (prototype assumption): $9.95 flat, free over $99 — TBC.

## The 12 order stages (customer-facing labels — identical for customers and staff)
1. **Submitted** — Order created, awaiting cards to arrive
2. **Received & inspected** — Package arrived, cards logged and photographed
3. **Quote ready** — Customer approval needed before work starts
4. **In restoration** — Active work — corners, surface, creases
5. **Restoration complete** — Before/after reveal available in the portal
6. **Prepped for grading** — Sleeved, documented, batched for submission
7. **Shipped to grader** — Sent to PSA / CGC / BGS / SGC
8. **At grader** — External checkpoint — status as available from the grader
9. **Graded — returned to us** — Card is back in-house
10. **Final QC & packaging** — Final check before return shipment
11. **Shipped to you** — Tracking number issued
12. **Delivered** — Carrier confirms delivery
- Restoration-only cards skip stages 6–9.
- Status copy tone: "Your card just went into restoration", not "Status: In Progress — Stage 4".
