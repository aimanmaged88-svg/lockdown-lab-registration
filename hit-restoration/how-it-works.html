Straight answers

What we won't pretend.

Trust runs both ways. Here's what we're upfront about.

We don't guarantee grades.

Grading decisions belong to the grading companies alone. Anyone promising you a grade is misleading you. We guarantee careful work and honest updates.

We don't guarantee an outcome.

Restoration is a skilled craft, not an exact science. Results depend on a card's condition and materials — inspection tells us what's realistic before you commit.

Restoration is disclosed on resale.

Standard hobby practice, and your responsibility when you sell. We're upfront about it because it protects everyone. Read the terms.
