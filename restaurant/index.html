The Heritage

Aged the old way,
by old-school hands.

At AGED, we do the one thing most kitchens gave up on: we wait. Whole cuts hang in our in-house chamber for weeks — up to ninety days — while time, salt air and cold do their slow work, concentrating the flavour into something you simply cannot fast-track.

It's the way beef was aged before the age of the shortcut. Our butchers learned it from the generation before them, and they'll show it to you through glass — the crust, the marbling, the patience on the hook.

Come hungry, and come curious. The best part of dinner here is understanding what you're about to eat.
