Our Story

Cooked over live fire,
rooted in the seasons.

Ember & Oak began with a single hearth and a simple belief: that great food is an event, not a transaction. Every plate that leaves our kitchen has met flame, smoke and patience — coaxed to its peak by hands that have cooked this way for a lifetime.

We work with a tight circle of farmers, foragers and fishers, letting the calendar write the menu. What you taste tonight may never appear in quite the same way again.
