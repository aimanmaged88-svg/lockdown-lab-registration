🚀 How to use the app

1
Sign in with your email No password. Tap the link in the email (or type the 6-digit code) and you're in.
2
Set up your trainer card Pick a name and emoji, list what you're offering and what you're hunting for.
3
Check the map Trainers near you show as pins — including the resident Gym Leaders (Ash, Misty, Brock and friends). Distances are approximate areas, never exact spots.
4
Propose a trade Tap Trade on any trainer, fill in "you give / you get", send. Gym Leaders answer within a minute — great for practice.
5
Chat & complete Once accepted, a chat opens to sort out the meetup. After the swap, hit Mark completed.
6
Browse the Pokédex The classic Kanto 151 with stats. Tap any Pokémon to add it to your offers or wants in one tap.

❓ FAQs

Does the app show my exact location?

Never. Your phone rounds your position to roughly a 500-metre area before it's sent, and the server rounds it again. There's no location history either — only your latest approximate area. Turn "Show me on the map" off and you're completely invisible.

Who are Ash, Misty and the other trainers already on the map?

They're our resident Gym Leaders — practice partners so you can try the whole trade flow end-to-end today. They accept proposals and chat back within a minute. Real trainers appear as more people join.

Does anything cost money?

No. There are no payments anywhere in the app — it's propose, chat, meet, swap.

I didn't get the sign-in email.

Give it a minute and check spam/junk. Still nothing? Tap "Use a different email" and try again. Each link works once — if you tapped an old one, just request a fresh email.

What phones does it work on?

Any phone (or computer) with a browser — it's a link, nothing to install. iPhone, Android, whatever you've got.

Can other people see my email or contact details?

No. Other trainers only ever see your trainer name, emoji, your offers/wants, and your approximate area if you share it. Contact happens in the in-app chat only.

How do I meet up safely?

Agree on a public spot in the chat — shopping centres, parks, the local courts. Daylight, public, easy. The trade isn't done until you both mark it completed.
